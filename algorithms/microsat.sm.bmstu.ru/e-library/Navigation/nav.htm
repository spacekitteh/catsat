GPS BOARD AND ANTENNA SPECIFICATIONS
 The science mission of the Cubesat is to obtain a profile of ionosphere plasma densities. One of the techniques will be GPS scintillation, where GPS signal strength at a point at two different times is compared. To achieve this, the satellite will be required to fly a GPS receiver and antenna. For simplification of systems, a two antenna configuration will be implemented.This specification establishes the design, construction, performance, development, and test requirements for the Cubesat GPS receiver, herein referred to as the GPS board.
